#Phone Review App
The phone review app, is a reactapplication that enables a user to add phone images and their specifications, like and unlike interesting phones and non interesting phones, edit and delete the phones from the list respectively.
 
 #FEATURES

 ThIS app is basically a free entertainment platform that has gat nice phone images and specs that enable one  to learn and understand different phones and their specifications giving them guidance in their future phone choices.

 USAGE

 the app is user friendly and interactive, with no prompts to sign up  for an account, all u need to do is search for the Apps name or use its link and enjoy the phone app world.
